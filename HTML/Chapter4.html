How do I stay socialized!




1. Have fun with your neighbor through the window and play a mime game!

This game goes like this: You choose who starts, then the first person mimes an action, animal or an object. The other person will try to guess what the first-person mime.

Afterward you switch and the second person mime and the first guess



2. Play board games with your family!

Boardgames is everything from chess with a board to card games.

You can even play a game where you draw parts of the object and your family can guess what you draw. The person drawing wins if no one guess the right before the sketch is done and get a win point. The family member who guesses right get 1-win point.

The important thing is to have fun and enjoy your time together!



3. You can ask your parents to borrow their phone!

Calling a friend or another family member.

You might get tired of the people around you but talking with someone else can help you getting some new topics for conversation with those around you.

Important! Calling can be expensive! So, remember to not talk to long!
