How do I take care of others without getting sick?




1. You can give the person sick water when they are empty!

You can use spring water or bottle water, depending what you have in the house



2. You can let the person who is sick get lots of rest!

You can help the sick person by tucking them in (in duvet or a blanket) and make sure they are good and warm!



3. A sick person needs to eat, in order to get healthy faster!

If you are big enough! You can help with making some simple food! Like bread with something on, you can ask what the sick person wants!
